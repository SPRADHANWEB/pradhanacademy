Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my purchased e-books?

After completing your purchase, you'll receive an email with download links for your e-books. You can also access them anytime by logging into your account on our website and visiting the "My E-Books" section in your dashboard.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), net banking, UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and popular digital wallets. All payments are processed through secure payment gateways.

Can I get a refund for my purchase?

We offer refunds within 7 days of purchase if you're not satisfied with our product. To request a refund, please contact our support team with your order details. Note that refunds are not available after you've downloaded the e-book or accessed significant course content.

How do live classes work?

Our live classes are conducted through a secure online platform. After enrollment, you'll receive a schedule and access link. Classes are interactive, allowing you to ask questions in real-time. If you miss a session, recorded versions will be available in your account within 24 hours.

Are the courses and e-books available in regional languages?

Yes, we offer many of our resources in multiple languages including Bengali, Hindi, and others. The available languages for each product are listed on its description page. We're continuously working to add more regional language content.
