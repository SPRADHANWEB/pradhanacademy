About Pradhan Academy

Empowering students through quality education and innovative learning solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Pradhan Academy began as a small initiative to help students in West Bengal prepare for their Class 12 board exams. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a full-fledged online learning platform serving thousands of students across India.

Our founder, Mr. Pradhan, recognized the challenges students face in accessing quality educational resources, especially in regional languages. With this vision, we set out to create affordable, accessible, and high-quality learning materials for students at all levels.

Today, we offer a comprehensive range of e-books, video courses, and live classes designed to make learning engaging and effective. Our team of expert educators is committed to helping students achieve their academic goals.

Our Mission

To democratize education by providing affordable, high-quality learning resources to students across India, with special focus on regional language content. We aim to bridge the gap between traditional classroom learning and modern digital education.

Our mission is to empower every student with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in their academic pursuits and beyond.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted online learning platform, recognized for our commitment to quality, affordability, and student success. We envision a future where every student, regardless of their location or economic background, has access to the best educational resources.

We strive to innovate continuously and adapt to the evolving needs of students in the digital age.
