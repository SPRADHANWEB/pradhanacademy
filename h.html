Help Center

Welcome to the Pradhan Academy Help Center. Here you can find answers to frequently asked questions and contact our support team for further assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

To enroll in a course, first, browse our "All Courses" section. Once you find a course you like, click on it to view the details. On the course details page, you'll see an "Enroll Now" or "Buy Now" button. Click this button and follow the prompts to complete your purchase and enrollment.

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), Net Banking, and popular UPI services. All payments are processed securely through our trusted payment gateway.

Our refund policy varies depending on the type of product (course or e-book) and the time since purchase. Please refer to our Refund Policy page for detailed information on eligibility and the refund process.

After purchasing an e-book, you can typically access it from your "My Library" or "My E-Books" section within your Pradhan Academy account. You may be able to download it in PDF format or read it directly on our platform.

Some courses may have prerequisites, while others are designed for beginners. Each course description page will clearly state any required prior knowledge or skills. Please check the course details before enrolling.

You can reach our customer support team via email or phone. Please see the "Contact Us" section below for our contact details.
